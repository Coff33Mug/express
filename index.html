Core mechanics

Dice

Players are given 6 dice to roll for points where:

100 points and 50 points respectively. Your goal as the player is to get as many points as possible! Although 100 and 50 points don't seem like much, it's possible to get combinations of dice for significantly larger amount of points! Read more about this in combinations.

Events

Alongside these dice, there are also special events! During your turn, you can press the event button to try your luck.

Special Event

There are events ranging from bonus points to completely losing your turn. High risk high reward!

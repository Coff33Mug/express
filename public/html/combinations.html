Combination Types

Dice adding

It's possible for two dice to be added to 5 and count for 50 points. This is especially useful for when you are required to fill.

2 + 3 and 1 + 4 is 50 points

Common Combinations

Triple #'s: # * 100 points

Triple 1's: 1000 points

Rare Combinations

Full hand of #'s: # * 500 points

Full hand of 1's: 5000 points

Straight: 1500 points

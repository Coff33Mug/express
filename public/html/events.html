Events

Extra Dice

In this event, you get an extra dice that counts towards your points! Those rare events seem way more possible now.

Wow! Without the extra dice you would not have gotten the extra 400 points.

Skip turn

You lose your turn... better luck next time.

Fill over 500 for 500

If you manage to fill your hand and that hand is worth over 500 points, you'll get 500 additional points!

Something like this will get you those bonus points.

Retribution

Fall behind? With this event, if you fill your hand, the player with the most points losses 3000 points! That's gotta suck for them!

Note:

No negative points! That'd be bad.

All or Nothing

Your turn isn't over until you bust! Unfortunately you lose the freedom to keep whatever dice you want... Dice of value will be automatically kept and you keep going til you can't.

Two for Two

Fill your hand twice and get double the points! You could keep an unfilled hand... but you wouldn't get any points.
